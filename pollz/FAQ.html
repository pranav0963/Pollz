    FAQ's

  • You can block users by:
    1. Clicking on the button.
    2. Then click on the Block User.


    You can unblock user by:
    1. Heading to the profile section by clicking on the in the home page.
    2. Click on the Edit profile button
    3. Click on the Blocked Users to get the list of users you have Blocked.
    4. Click on the unblock button corresponding to the user.

  • You can start conversations by:
    1. Clicking on the or by Heading to the profile section by clicking on thein the home page and then clicking on the chat button.
    2. You can share texts as well as images by clicking on the button.


    You can delete chats by:
    1. Clicking on the button.

  • You can create new poll by:
    1. Clicking on the button
    2. Give a short description to your poll.
    3. Upload pictures which you would like to include in your poll.
    4. Enter the Question you want to publish.
    5. Enter the options related to your poll.
    6. Hit the publish button.

  • You can delete your Account by
    1. Heading to the profile section by clicking on the in the home page.
    2. Click on the Edit profile button
    3. Click on the Delete Account button(Please note that data can not be retreived).

  • You can Edit your Profile Picture by:
    1. Heading to the profile section by clicking on the in the home page.
    2. Click on the Edit profile button
    3. Upload your photo
    4. Click on the Save button.

  • You can delete a poll by:
    1. Heading to the profile section by clicking on the in the home page.
    2. Clicking on the button on the poll.

  • You can like a poll by:
    1. Clicking on the button.

    You can comment on a poll by:
    1. Clicking on the button.
    2. You can also leave replies to another comment.

    You can share a poll externally by:
    1. Clicking on the button.

  • You can Login by:
    1. Clicking on the Login button(Provided you have already signed up).
    2. Enter your Email and password.
    3. Click on the Login button.

  • You can Logout by:
    1. Heading to the profile section by clicking on the in the home page.
    2. Click on the button.

  • You can maintain poll counts by posting polls daily to keep the current poll count going. A days break sets the current poll count to 0. The maximum current poll count acheived will be the highest poll count.

  • You can search for either polls(Topics) or Users by:
    1. Heading to the search section by clicking on the in the home page
    2.Move to the User search or the Topic search

  • You can Signup by:
    1. Clicking on the Signup button while using the application for the first time.
    2. Enter the details(Email, Password, Full name)
    3. Choose a unique Username.
    4. Verify your account by entering the OTP sent to your Mail.

  • You can Edit your Profile Picture by:
    1. Heading to the profile section by clicking on the in the home page.
    2. Click on the Edit profile button
    3. Edit your name and bio.
    4. Click on the Save button.

  • You can now get verified on POLLZ and acquire the POLLER badge:
    1. Early birds who are students from various institutes and influencers from Instagram and X who are already verified are welcome to take the badge provided they satisfy the criteria.
    2. Regular users are eligible for the badge if they have a HIGHEST POLL COUNT of 100.
